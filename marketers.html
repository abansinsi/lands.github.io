REAL ESTATE BUSINESS FOR EVERYONE

Welcome to LandsandHousing.com Online Marketers Information Page.

The Online Marketer is an arm of the MasterMind Marketer’s Program of LandsandHousing.com. It is designed to give everyone the opportunity to participate in the business of real estate which up until now had been the exclusive preserve of the professionals in the field.


The internet, in any field of human endeavour has been to democratized (liberate) the body of knowledge in that field so that as many people as want to can benefit from the oppurtunities in it while also bearing the associated risk inherent in the field. eg.


  1. Forex, before the internet was only being traded by banks and big multi-national firms. But with the internet, anyone can acquired the information required, trade and make profit from forex.

  2. Google as a company has democratized information i.e. makes information available to everyone without any prejudice.

The real estate business, at the very basic level is an information business.

  1. Information acquired.
  2. Information dispensed.

Therefore, when someone says, e.g. I’m a realtor (agent), he/she means is I have acquired the information (knowledge) required to successfully execute a real estate transaction.

As you go further, you shall also acquire the information that shall empower you to successful execute real estate transactions.
